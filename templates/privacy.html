Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the information you provide when you create an account (such as your name and email address) and the expense data you enter while using Spendly. We do not collect financial account credentials or payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate and improve Spendly — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and keep your account secure. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Cookies

Spendly uses session cookies strictly necessary for authentication. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us through the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice within the app or by email.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the contact information provided within the app.
